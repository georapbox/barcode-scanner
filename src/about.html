About



The average American throws away around $2000 of edible food every year because of spoilage. As a nation it is estimated around 408 billion USD in groceries is thrown away every year. To combat the insanely high food waste problem in the U.S., we developed an app that keeps track of all food items you bring into your home and suggest recipes based on what you have available, what will expire soon, and what your preferences are.
