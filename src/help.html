Help & Information

About This Project

  • Barcode Scanner App - A Progressive Web Application (PWA) for scanning product barcodes using your device's camera
  • Real-time Detection - Uses the native Barcode Detection API to identify barcodes instantly without server requests
  • Offline Support - Works completely offline thanks to service worker technology; no internet required for core scanning features
  • 7-Day History - All scanned items are automatically saved with a 7-day expiration countdown for easy tracking
  • Multiple Input Methods - Scan via webcam in real-time or upload barcode images for offline processing
  • Product Information - Automatic product lookup displays ingredients, nutritional data, and allergen information
  • Local Security - Your data stays on your device; optional account management with secure authentication
  • Cross-Platform - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers with responsive design
  • Flash Control - Toggle device flash/torch for better barcode visibility in low light conditions
  • Customizable Settings - Control notifications, effects (beep/vibrate), and supported barcode formats

How to Use

Scanning with Webcam

  1. Click Use webcam on the home page
  2. Allow camera access when prompted by your browser
  3. Point your camera at a product barcode
  4. The app will automatically detect and scan the barcode
  5. Product information will appear below

Uploading an Image

  1. Click Use image tab
  2. Drag and drop a barcode image, or click to select a file
  3. The barcode will be scanned automatically
  4. Results will display below the upload area

Features

History Tracking

All scanned items are saved to your history with a 7-day expiration. Click the History button (clock icon) to view past scans.

Account Management

Create an optional account to sync your scans across devices. Click the Account button (person icon) to sign up or sign in.

Settings

Customize your scanning experience by clicking the Settings button (gear icon). You can control:

  • Automatic web page opening
  • History saving preferences
  • Continuous scanning mode
  • Sound and vibration effects
  • Supported barcode formats

Troubleshooting

  • Camera not working? Make sure your browser has permission to access the camera. Check your browser settings.
  • Can't scan a barcode? Ensure good lighting and hold the camera steady. Try adjusting the distance from the barcode.
  • No product information? The barcode may not be in our database. Try scanning a different product.
  • History not saving? Check that history saving is enabled in Settings.

Need more help? Check the FAQ or contact us for additional support.
