# Quick Start - Just Want to Scan?

Here's all you need to know:

## 1. Open the App

Go to: **https://barcode-scanner-cs465.web.app**

## 2. Sign In

- Click "Account" (top right)
- Click "Continue Without Account"

## 3. Turn On Saving

- Click "Settings" (gear icon)
- Check ✓ "Add to history"

## 4. Start Scanning

- Click "Use webcam"
- Allow camera when asked
- Point at barcode
- Done!

## 5. See Your Scans

Click "History" (clock icon) anytime to see everything you scanned.

---

## That's It!

Everything saves automatically. Works on your phone. No app download needed.

**The URL again**: https://barcode-scanner-cs465.web.app

Bookmark it or add to home screen!

---

## Problems?

**Nothing working?** → Someone needs to turn on authentication first. Check SETUP_GUIDE_FOR_TEAM.md

**Can't scan?** → Allow camera access when your browser asks

**Where's my history?** → Make sure "Add to history" is checked in Settings

---

Got it working? Great! Now you can scan anything.

Your scans stay for 30 days on your phone and forever in the cloud.
